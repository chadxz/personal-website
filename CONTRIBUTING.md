# Contributing

## Writing for this site

This is Chad's personal website. A consistent tone across posts is a non-goal.
Do not normalize posts into one house style or sand down a piece until it
sounds like every other post.

The goal is authenticity. Speak directly to the audience for the piece about
topics they are likely to care about. Some posts will be technical, some will
be reflective, some will be rougher around the edges, and that variety is good.
There is variety in life, and the writing on this site can have variety too.

Avoid prose that feels pretentious, formulaic, preachy, or like a generic
thought-leadership template. Prefer concrete experience, clear judgment, and
plain language over tidy lessons or universal claims.

When editing an existing post, preserve the post's natural voice unless Chad
asks for a rewrite. Improve clarity and flow, but do not make every post sound
as if it came from the same template.
